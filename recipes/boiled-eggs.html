Boiled Eggs

People boil eggs because it's an easy and fast way to cook them, making them simple to peel and eat. Boiled eggs are healthy and can be eaten by themselves, in salads, or in different dishes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a pot, not too tight, They should rest in comfort, just right.
  2. Fill with cold water, an inch above, Cover the eggs with a gentle shove.
  3. On high heat, let the water boil strong, Rapidly bubbling, it won't take long.
  4. Once boiling, cover, and off the heat, Set a timer, your desired time to meet.
  5. Prepare a bowl with ice and cold water, For when the eggs need to cool later.
  6. When the time is up, with tongs be swift, Into the ice water, give them a lift.
  7. Tap, peel, and rinse the eggs with care,Pat them dry, they're ready to share.
